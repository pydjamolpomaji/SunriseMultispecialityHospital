OBSTETRICS & GYNAECOLOGY

Dr. Kalpana Gupta

Consultant Obstetrics & Gynecology
MBBS, DNB, MNAMS, Fellowship in infertility(FOGSI)

Dr. Kalpana Gupta is practicing as an Obstetrician & Gynecologist since past 12 years. Her Vision of women and her health needs lead to Cradle Clinic which approaches women as a whole be it pregnancy, reproductive or sexual health issues. All of her needs are catered under one roof. She is an expert in managing complicated deliveries, high-risk Cesarean Section, Infertility Treatment(all kinds), Hysterectomy, PCOD treatment, and Laparoscopic Surgeries.

Dr. Kalpana did her Post Graduate training in Obstetrics and Gynecology from prestigious Sir Ganga Ram Hospital, New Delhi. She is a Gold Medalist. She worked at Nowrosjee Wadia Maternity Hospital under Dr. M.S. Nanavati. Dr. Kalpana Gupta is a member of the Federation of Obstetric and Gynaecological Societies of India (FOGSI), AOGD and Navi Mumbai Obstetric & Gynecological Society (NMOGS).
