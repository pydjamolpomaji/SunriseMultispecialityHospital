Haematology & Oncology

Dr. Govind Kendre

Consultant Haematology
MBBS, MD(Paediatrics), DM(Clinical Hematology)

Dr. Govind Kendre is a Super-specialist in the field of Clinical Haematology. He has completed his DM in Clinical Haematology from Seth G.S. Medical College, Mumbai in the year 2018. He has completed his MD in Paediatric Medicine from LTMMC, Sion, Mumbai in the year 2014. Further, he has also gained experience in Bone Marrow Transplant from TATA Memorial Centre, ACTREC, Mumbai.

He is brilliant in his thought process in diagnosing complicated cases and treating different types of haematology cases with special interests in acute & chronic Leukemia, Lymphoma, multiple myeloma, aplastic anemia, haemophilia, thalassemia, sickle cell disease, ITP& DVT.

Key Skills & Experience : Dr. Govind Kendre has vast experience in treating patients of all Haematology related cases. He has been handling patients of leukemia, lymphoma, different types of anemias & bleeding disorders. After completion of DM, he has worked as an assistant Professor of Clinical Hematology at KEM Hospital, Mumbai for 2 years with teaching experience and research activity.

He is proficient in performing bone marrow aspiration & biopsy procedure, Lumbar Puncture, Central Vein catheterization, Exchange transfusion and scheduling appropriate Chemotherapy.His clinical judgment and analytical abilities are exceptional.

Area Of Expertise

  • Blood Cancer
  • Bleeding Disorder
  • Bone marrow aspiration and biopsy procedure
  • Impractical Chemotherapy
  • All blood diseases
