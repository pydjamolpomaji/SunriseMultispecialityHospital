Dr. Vinod Pachade is a renowned plastic and reconstructive and cosmetic surgeon in Navi Mumbai with more than 10 yrs of experience He completed his education from the prestigious Grant Government Medical college Mumbai. Right from the training period, he has got an immense interest in the “cosmetic world” of plastic surgery due to his ability to see and analyze finer details in the shape and beauty of the human body.

Due to specialized training in hair transplant and body contouring surgery, he has successfully performed many hair transplantations and tummy tuck surgery (abdominoplasty) till date. He does all types of breast surgeries including breast implants, tightening of breast and reduction of the breast. Liposuction and facial cosmetic procedure are the most common surgeries in his routine practice. He is associated with the number of leading hospitals across the city.
